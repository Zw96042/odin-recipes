Lasagna

Description

A baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients
  • Pasta dough
  • Lasagna filling (cheese, veggies)
  • Lasagna sauce
Steps
  1. Role out the dough
  2. Cut the dough
  3. Put the filling inside a dough pocket
  4. Cook the lasagna
  5. Put sauce on the lasagna
  6. done!!!
